Hey, how are you?

My darlings, Ruby is magical!

Here was I, playing a little, learning a lot and BOOM, just got blown away by something super cool: adding and subtracting arrays; but you may ask "What you mean by that?". I show you.

[code lang=ruby]
a = [1, 2]
b = [3, 4]
c = a + b

p c
[/code]

The out:

[1,2,3,4]

Yep! The concatenation of the arrays!!!

The subtracting may also be done;

[code lang=ruby]
a = [1, 2]
b = [3, 4]
c = a + b

p c-a
[/code]

Out:

[3,4]

Oh my! Ruby always surprises me!
